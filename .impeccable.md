## Design Context

### Users
English teachers and students use this tool for classroom projection, quick drills, and personal review. The interface must be easy to scan from a large screen and fast to operate during practice.

### Brand Personality
Clean, focused, and steady. The product should feel like a refined learning tool, not a marketing landing page or a decorative template.

### Aesthetic Direction
Keep the current warm paper background, black text, brown/gold accent color, real logo in the top-left, and restrained bottom waves on the home screen. Visual changes should polish spacing, typography, states, and responsive behavior without changing the identity.

### Design Principles
- Preserve the existing calm paper-and-ink style.
- Make buttons and controls simple, stable, and classroom-friendly.
- Prioritize readability, keyboard use, and clear answer states.
- Use decorative elements sparingly, only where they support the brand.
- Avoid generic AI patterns such as oversized cards, loud gradients, and needless visual effects.
